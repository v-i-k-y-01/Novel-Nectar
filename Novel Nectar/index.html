Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert T. Kiyosaki is a personal finance classic that challenges conventional views on money and investing. The author shares his life lessons and financial wisdom through the contrasting philosophies of his "rich dad" (his friend's father) and his "poor dad" (his biological father). Kiyosaki emphasizes the importance of financial education, the difference between assets and liabilities, and the value of entrepreneurship.
